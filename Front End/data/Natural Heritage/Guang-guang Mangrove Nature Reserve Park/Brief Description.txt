The Guang-guang Mangrove Nature Reserve Park is a 201-hectare ecological sanctuary in Barangay Dahican. A vital part of the Pujada Bay Protected Landscape, with 19 mangrove species that protects and serves as a natural defense against storm surges and erosion. Visitors can explore via scenic boardwalks and visit the Environmental and Ecotourism Center. As a "cleaner and greener" landmark of Mati, it offers a unique opportunity to witness the rich marine biodiversity and local conservation efforts.
