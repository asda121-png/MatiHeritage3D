Waniban Island is a pristine four-hectare islet situated within the protected waters of Pujada Bay and is one of the three primary destinations forming the "Tri-Island" hopping circuit of the City of Mati. Located just a short boat ride from Barangay Tamisan, the island is characterized by a stunning contrast between its powdery white coral sand beaches and a lush, verdant interior.
The surrounding marine environment is defined by exceptionally clear turquoise waters, offering high visibility for underwater exploration. The northern side of the island is particularly notable for its vibrant coral gardens and steep underwater drop-offs, establishing it as a premier location for snorkeling and scuba diving. Additionally, the island serves as a natural barrier within the bay, ensuring moderate to calm wave conditions that enhance its appeal as a peaceful recreational sanctuary
