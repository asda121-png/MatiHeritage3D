Pujada Island is a stunning 157-hectare natural sanctuary and the largest islet in the Pujada Bay. Known for its lush forests, hidden white-sand coves, and vibrant coral reefs, it is a centerpiece of the City of Mati’s environmental heritage. As a protected landscape, it offers visitors a raw encounter with nature, providing a serene escape where the deep blue of the Pacific meets the rich biodiversity of Davao Oriental.
