Point Zero of Davao Oriental

The Pylon Monument stands on JP Rizal Street, Barangay Central, as Mati’s premier cultural landmark. Designed by National Artist Juan F. Nakpil and later renovated with culturally themed ornamentation, it remains a symbol of the city’s faith and resilience.

  • 30-foot reinforced concrete & brick tower
  • Central glass sphere from recycled San Miguel bottles
  • Recognized under Resolution No. 61, Series of 2019
